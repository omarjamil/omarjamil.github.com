Travel

North Devon is quite remote, so it can be a challenge to get to Ilfracombe (see map here)

By car

This is probably the easiest way to get to Ilfracombe. There are only two roads that come into the town: A361 (from Barnstaple) and A399 (from Combe Martin). Once you're in the town, there are brown tourist signs that will guide you to Tunnels Beaches. The full venue address is below.

There is a pay-and-display car park outside the venue, which will hold about 35 cars. Ilfracombe is a small town and easy to walk around, so if you are staying locally it would be great if you could leave your car (if possible) at your accommodation. This will help us free up parking spaces for evening guests.

By Train

Unfortunately, there is no train station in Ilfracombe. The nearest train station is in Barnstaple, which is approximately 12 miles away.

Buses do run frequently between Barnstaple and Ilfracombe and take about 45mins. The Stagecoach 21/21A service leaves from outside Barnstaple train station and drops off on Ilfracombe seafront. The cost for a single ticket is about Â£3.50.
