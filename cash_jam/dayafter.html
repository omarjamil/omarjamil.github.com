The Day After

We appreciate that due to the distances many of you will have to travel, you may need to head back home quite early on the Sunday. However, if you have a little more time to spare, we thought we should try and make the most of some of the awesome beaches that the North Devon coast has to offer. The plan, weather permitting, is to go to Woolacombe Beach, about 10mins drive from Ilfracombe (there's also a bus that runs between the two and Barnstaple). Let's meet at The Tides Inn, Beach Road, Woolacombe, Devon, EX34 7BP 12 pm onwards.

We will try and bring some beach games along, but for the less active, we will also have beer from the fledgling Cashmore-Jamil Brewery to keep you entertained.

If, in the unlikely event, the weather isn't beach friendly, we will try and find a cafe/pub to hang out in. Either way, we can do something fun!
